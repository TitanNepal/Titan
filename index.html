About TITAN

Our Mission

The IPM Trainers Association Nepal (TITAN) was established in 2002 with a mission to promote Integrated Pest Management (IPM) and sustainable agricultural practices through farmer field schools across Nepal.

Currently, TITAN is implementing 60 Natural Resource Management Field Schools in Koshi Province under the "Building a Resilient Churia Region in Nepal" (BRCRN) project funded by the Green Climate Fund (GCF) and implemented by FAO in partnership with the Ministry of Forests and Environment.

Our work focuses on climate-resilient land use practices, forest ecosystem management, and building resilience to climate-induced hazards in vulnerable river systems.
