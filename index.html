Who We Are

Building Climate-Resilient Communities Since 2002

TITAN is a professional network of experts dedicated to promoting Integrated Pest Management (IPM) and sustainable agriculture across Nepal. Established with the vision of empowering farmers and communities, TITAN has been at the forefront of designing, implementing, and scaling farmer-centered training programs.

Over the years, TITAN has collaborated with national and international organizations to develop curricula, conduct trainings, and implement field-based solutions for sustainable natural resource management.

🌾 Farmer Field Schools (FFS) & NRM Field Schools across multiple districts.
📋 Contributed to national IPM & Pesticide Policy drafts with the Department of Agriculture.
🔬 Training on biological control, bio-pesticides, and eco-friendly crop protection.
📗 Published manuals including the Coffee Production & Marketing Manual.
🦋 Pheromone-based pest control demonstrations across Asia and Africa.

Our Work

Projects & Field Programmes

From Koshi Province's river systems to GCF-funded climate resilience projects, our work spans communities, ecosystems, and generations.

ONGOING

NRM Field Schools in Koshi Province

Operating 60 Natural Resource Management Field Schools across 16 river systems, promoting climate-resilient land use practices in Koshi Province.

GCF FUNDED

Building a Resilient Churia Region

Working with FAO and MoFE on Nepal's first full-scale GCF funded project across 26 river systems in Koshi, Madhesh, and Bagmati Provinces.

CAPACITY BUILDING

Farmer Training & Capacity Building

Conducting training programs on sustainable agriculture, forest management, and climate-resilient practices through the field school approach.

NRM Field School Orientation — Itahari
